Poem : I am still dance !

I am still dance

I can see you watching me

How hypnotized you become watching my tree trunk legs

Spin

And spin

Until you see roots of childhood insults

And the distorted voices trying to whisper hurtful things

I swear i heard your voice too

But Don’t you know?

I am too stubborn

Too proud of tree trunk legs making rings out of spins

How I am sun

Be careful not to get burned from this passionate round

Be careful not to blind yourself with my bright eyes

Round and filled with wonder

Like how you are so transfixed with how my body moves

Don’t you know trees sway too?

That sun bodies can make heat wave?

That my hips are a river that keeps flowing and swaying

To a tempo you can only dream of flowing along to

I see you amused

Looking at me like I’m crazy

How dare me dance with my round

Love this sun shaped body

And tree trunk legs

But sweetie I am mother nature

My body is made up of the most beautiful things

And you are just a visitor

A guest hypnotized by my sway

By my round

By stomps and kicks

You can never keep up with

DOn’t you know?

When i first heard your distorted whisper

Caress my ear with a message that rolled off your axe tongue

My tree trunk legs only spun faster

Not caring about distorted whispers and axe tongues

Don’t you know?

No matter how many voices try to tell me no

I am still mother nature

I am still dance

And this round still transfixes you

With beauty and a sway only you

Can dream to sway to
